About Gazipur

GazipurDistrict is an administrative region of Dhaka Division in central Bangladesh . Due to its location, it is a special class district of Bangladesh. [2] Gazipur district is full of immense potential in the combination of history and tradition with its radiant splendor and colorful brilliance. The first armed resistance war took place in Gazipur on 19 March 1971 at the beginning of the Great War of Liberation. Gazipur is home to 19 KPIs, 5 universities, 1 cantonment and the country's only high-tech park with a large number of government, autonomous, non-governmental organizations and a large part of the country's readymade garment industry including small / medium and heavy industries. Gazipur City CorporationIt is the largest city corporation of Bangladesh with an area of ​​330 km2. Gazipur is home to 75% of the country's garment industry. The second largest gathering in the Muslim world, the World Ijtema , was held on the banks of the Turag River in Tongi. Bhawal Garh, the largest and nationally recognized national park in Bangladesh, and Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Safari Park, the largest safari park in Asia, are located in Gazipur. The only mint or money making factory in Bangladesh , where all the money of the country is made, is located at Gazipur. The only arms factory in Bangladesh is located at Gazipur. Kashimpur Central Jail, the largest and most important of the 13 Central Jails in Bangladesh, is located at Gazipur. Bangladesh Agricultural Research Institute at Gazipur ,Bangladesh Rice Research Institute and Bangladesh Cotton Research Institute are locate.
